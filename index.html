Site Introduction

Sharks below, sharks to the right and sharks above!

Throughout this art-based website you will find shark related content in several different media. The various sharks were created or documented by Nicholas Amerongen. The reason being that sharks are such a fascinating subject that learning about them gives inspiration to creative outlets to represent them.
